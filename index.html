First Responders Network is a non- profit organization whose interest is based on social justice and community outreach. We are focused on ensuring there is educational ,social and economic equity in communities around us. We engage ourselves in youth initiatives, providing food, housing and other essentials to those in need making the world a better place.

We believe in the power of groups and as a result of this our main aim is to target those who have been marginalized. We are highly centered around educational equity in practice and policy reforms to which our motto is "First Responders Network...we reach, educate, and transform lives".

We are strong and reliable and can do far more than expected to combat the social ills that plague our community. At First Responders Network we nurture and unleash solutions that transform lives.
